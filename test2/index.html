About

The Band

Formed in 2006 in Lugano, Switzerland. Since their inception they have never been an ordinary Metal band. Their mix of melody, heaviness and uplifting lyrics gained the attention of the modern Rock, Metal and Hardcore scenes.

After the release of their first EP "To The Edge Of Reality" in 2008 they signed to Universal Music / Spinefarm Records and then released the albums "What Silence Hides" in 2011 and "The Gift Of Life" in 2013, which features evergreen songs like "Consumed Future", "Photographs" and "Your Voice". The band toured continuously and touched 4 continents: Europe, North America, Asia and Africa.

In 2015 they released "Dreamers Don't Sleep", the first single anticipating a new album. The band signed with Artery Recordings in 2016 and released "Vibrant" featuring new influences from Active Rock, Electronic and Rap music — followed by a sold-out Headliner tour through Europe and Asia.

"Question Everything" was released on January 15th 2019, and the band started a World Tour with DON BROCO & Emarosa through Europe, South Africa and Asia.

The album "A Pale Blue Dot" was released on March 5th 2021 and features "Stone Cold Digital", "Shanghai Nights" and "Safe Harbour". In 2022 the single "Rise" launched the band's return to the festival circuit, including Greenfield Festival (CH) and Metal Capital Festival (FI).
